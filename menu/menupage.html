  • What is vegetarian?

    India has the world's largest number of vegetarians. Many communities in India have always been vegetarian. Many Indians who do not regularly follow a vegetarian diet may adopt one during religious festivals. Also, many Indian vegetarians eschew eggs as well as meat.However, all consume dairy products.Veganism is generally not well known in India.Each of India's 29 states provides different, yet popular vegetarian cuisines.
